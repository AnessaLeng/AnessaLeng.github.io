House of Leng

Over ten years ago, a brave couple from Cambodia, escaping the terror of civil war, founded a distinctive Chinese restaurant in our city. Their journey, fueled by a shared love for cooking and unwavering hope, led to the transformation of a modest eatery into a cherished local establishment. The restaurant now stands as a beacon of resilience, embodying the triumph over hardship, and the fusion of diverse cultures. Welcome to a dining experience where food speaks the universal language of resilience.
